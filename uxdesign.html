>> ACT Question of the Day (Chegg)

Design of a product which delivers daily sample ACT questions, as well as archived practice questions organized by topic. I worked on each step of the design process, from competitive research, to wireframes, to numerous iterations of designs and layouts. I focused on utilizing SEO strategies (such as internal linking and indexation), as well as cohesive design elements to integrate the product with the rest of the site. The designs were to be used as a template for all future SEO-focused content.

>> Study Snacks (Chegg)

An interactive page which allows users to find recipes and ideas which fit their tastes/moods by sorting based on user-selected filters. For this project I wrote copy text, researched and selected content (recipes), and designed the user interface. The main goal of this project was creating an engaging user experience to increase time on site, quality content, and external linking.
